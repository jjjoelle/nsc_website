title: Surrogate Gradient Methods for Spiking Neural Network Optimization

student: marco-rossi
abstract: >
  A comparative study of surrogate gradient approaches for training spiking neural networks on event-based vision benchmarks. We evaluate fast sigmoid, SuperSpike, and multi-Gaussian surrogates on DVS-Gesture and N-MNIST, finding that multi-Gaussian surrogates achieve the best accuracy-efficiency tradeoff.
